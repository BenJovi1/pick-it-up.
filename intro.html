Types of guitars

There are many different types of guitars, but the most common are acoustic, electric, and classical.
Each type has its own unique sound and feel.

Acoustic guitars

Acoustic guitars are the most common type of guitar. They are typically made of wood and have a hollow
body that amplifies the sound. They are great for beginners because they are easy to play and don't
require any additional equipment.

Electric guitars

Electric guitars are typically made of solid wood and require an amplifier to produce very much sound.
They have a thinner neck and lighter strings, making them easier to play for some people. They are
great for playing rock, metal, and other genres that require a louder sound.

Classical guitars

Classical guitars are similar to acoustic guitars but have a wider neck and nylon strings. They are
typically used for playing classical music and fingerstyle techniques. They have a softer, mellower
sound compared to acoustic and electric guitars.
